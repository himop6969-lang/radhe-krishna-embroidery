About Us

Radhe Krishna Embroidery is a dedicated machine embroidery unit located in Faridabad, Haryana. We specialize exclusively in computerized machine embroidery job-work for garment exporters and manufacturers.

Our facility is equipped with advanced multi-head embroidery machines designed for high-precision, bulk production. We focus entirely on the embroidery process, ensuring uniform quality and timely output for large-scale orders. We deliver exact machine precision that meets strict industrial standards.

Our Services

Dori Embroidery

Computerized coiling and cording embroidery executed on multi-head machines for consistent pattern reproduction.

Sequence Embroidery

High-speed automated sequence attachment, capable of multi-size sequins and complex overlapping designs.

Zari Embroidery

Machine-finished Zari work providing a premium metallic look with the speed and accuracy of computerized stitching.

Custom Digitizing

Professional digitizing services to convert vector art into precise machine-readable embroidery files.

Bulk Embroidery Production

Dedicated production lines for large volume job-work, ensuring fast turnaround for deadlines.
